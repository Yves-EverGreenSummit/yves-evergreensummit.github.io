About Me

I’m Yves Deutschmann, founder of EverGreen Summit Data Solutions, and I specialize in helping small and midsize manufacturers take their first steps towards energy efficiency and ESG compliance. Unlike traditional solutions, I offer a highly flexible approach that doesn’t require large initial investments. I implement practical systems that quickly demonstrate value and adapt to grow alongside my clients’ businesses.

What sets me apart is my 15+ years of hands-on experience in both energy management and software implementation. I don’t just provide tools; I offer expertise that integrates seamlessly with whatever systems a company already has in place, focusing on achievable actions that make an immediate impact. This approach is particularly valuable for SMEs that can’t afford full-time energy management personnel but still want to improve efficiency and comply with ESG standards.
